Fortunately, there are some alternatives that can help to reduce our exposure to harmful plastic chemicals. We can consider drinking water using:

Clean Water Action

The Clean Water Action is an organization that takes action to protect drinking water and clean up polluted waterways. Through campaigns, education, and legislative efforts, Clean Water Action strives to promote sustainable water management practices and ensure that everyone has access to clean and safe water.
